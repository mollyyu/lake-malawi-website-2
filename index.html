Pioneering the post-Sustainability future - today

Treating Lake Malawi as the unified ecosystem it truly is -

Positive Development™ moves beyond "doing less harm" by creating
systems where nature, society and local economies regenerate together

At Lake Malawi, we are co-creating a world-first Living Lab that links regenerative farming,
sustainable aquaculture, circular SMEs, higher education, ecotourism, public health and community-
led conservation

The goal is simple: engineer positive tipping points that strengthen ecosystems, improve livelihoods
and demonstrate what a post-Sustainability future can look like

Only one country can be first - and Malawi is poised to lead the world
